Google Fonts is a collection of free, open-source fonts that you can use on your website. These fonts are carefully curated and optimized for web use, making it easy to enhance the typography of your web pages.

One popular font from Google Fonts is Montserrat. It's a versatile sans-serif font that can be used for a wide range of design projects. Montserrat is known for its clean and modern look, making it a great choice for both headers and body text.
